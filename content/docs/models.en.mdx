## Data models

Swell offers a collection of ready-to-use models, designed to cover the common needs of a modern e-commerce project.

These models serve as a solid foundation, but remain entirely customizable to adapt to the structure and specificities
of your application.

You can thus modify them or create new ones according to your business domain requirements,
while benefiting from smooth integration with the Swell ecosystem.

## Adapting a model

**Swell** models are basically located in the `app/Models` folder, except for
optional features whose models are placed in `app/Modules/Feature/Models`.

They are associated with their respective migration files in `database/migrations` or
`Modules/Feature/database/migrations`, allowing you to define the structure of corresponding tables.

<Callout>
  Swell imposes no business logic. The proposed models serve as an initial
  foundation, to be freely adapted. For any structure or behavior modification,
  refer to the official [Laravel](https://laravel.com/docs/12.x/eloquent)
  documentation.
</Callout>
